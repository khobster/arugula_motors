White Bread

A point-of-sale system for pizza, sandwich, and counter shops.
Owned, not rented.

Toast is bleeding you out.

Toast charges $69–$165 a month per terminal — before you turn it on. Then $50 a month per software module: online ordering, gift cards, inventory, payroll. Then 2.49–3.5% plus 15¢ on every transaction. Most small pizza shops are paying $400 or more a month, every month, on top of card fees they can't escape because Toast hardware only talks to Toast software.

You came up with the recipe. You found the suppliers. You make the dough. The software that takes the order shouldn't be the thing eating your margin.

White Bread does the same job. You pay for it once. You own it. The hardware is whatever tablet you already have.

What White Bread does

  • Order entry with modifiers (sizes, toppings, customizations) on any tablet or laptop.
  • Kitchen tickets — print to your existing ticket printer or display on a screen.
  • Stripe payments with the lowest card-processing fees in the industry.
  • Inventory deduction per sale — every pie reduces your flour, cheese, sauce, box counts.
  • Restaurant Depot shopping list auto-generated from current stock + your forecast.
  • Dough fermentation calendar — tells you what to mix today for tomorrow, Friday, the weekend.
  • Daily, weekly, monthly reports — sales, top items, cost of goods, real margin per pie.
  • Multiple terminals if you grow — same software, no per-terminal licensing.
  • Receipt printing and a cash drawer trigger if you want them.
