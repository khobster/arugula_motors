How we get found by AI

We applied Beacon to our own site, Arugula Motors, and we track it every week. This is the honest before and after.

More people skip the search bar now and just ask AI for the answer. They ask ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google Gemini for the best spot near them, and they trust the short list that comes back. If your business is not on that list, you do not exist to them. It is like not being on page one of Google, except now there is no page two.

Search is not what it was

And it is not just chatbots. Google itself now answers most questions right at the top of the page with AI, so people read the answer and never click through to a website. The old game was ranking on a list of blue links. The new game is being inside the answer.
