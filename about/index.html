About

Arugula Motors is one person, in Charleston.

I'm Kevin, and I build custom websites, software, and dashboards for local businesses here in Charleston, South Carolina.

The idea is simple. Most small businesses rent their software from big SaaS companies and pay every month, forever. I build you the same thing as a custom tool you own outright, so the monthly bleed stops.

I build some of it for myself too. White Bread, my point of sale system, runs Caro's, my pop-up pizzeria. So when something is annoying, I find out the same day my customers do.

Questions

What does Arugula Motors do?

Arugula Motors builds custom websites, software, and dashboards for local businesses in Charleston, South Carolina. You own what we build, instead of renting it from a SaaS company.

Where is Arugula Motors based?

Charleston, South Carolina. We work with local businesses across the Lowcountry, and remotely on software projects anywhere.

What is Beacon?

Beacon gets your business found when customers ask AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google's AI for a recommendation. It covers GEO and AEO, and we track your visibility every month. It costs $1,500 to set up and $300 a month, cancel anytime.

What is White Bread?

White Bread is a point of sale system for pizza, sandwich, and counter shops. You pay once and own it, instead of paying Toast every month per terminal. A single shop build is $22,000.

Do I own what you build?

Yes. With our software products you get the source code and full ownership on delivery. No subscriptions, no per terminal fees, no lock-in. Beacon is the exception, since it is an ongoing monthly service.

Who builds it?

Kevin, the founder. He builds and runs the software himself, in Charleston, South Carolina.
