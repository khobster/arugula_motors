Broom

Cleaning and turnover software for short-term rentals.
Owned, not rented.

If you run short-term rentals, you know the bleed.

Turno charges $8–$25 per property per month. Properly the same. Breezeway adds setup fees on top of $15–$30 per property per month. A 30-property portfolio is paying $400–$700 a month, every month, forever — to software that schedules cleaners, verifies photos, and emails owners.

Broom does the same job. You pay for it once. You own it. You can take the code and walk out the door any time.

What Broom does

  • Property list with cleaning checklists customized per property.
  • Cleaner assignment — auto or manual, based on availability and zone.
  • Photo verification on every clean, captured from the cleaner's phone.
  • Auto-pay your cleaners via Stripe ACH when jobs are marked complete.
  • Owner dashboard — today's turns, statuses, supply orders, damage reports.
  • Airbnb + VRBO calendar sync — cleanings triggered automatically by check-out.
  • SMS notifications to cleaners and owners. No app downloads for anyone.
  • Mobile-first — works on any phone, installs to home screen with one tap.
